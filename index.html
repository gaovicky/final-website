Zoom Fatigue

What is it?

Virtual meetings have been a great way for people to socialize while following social distancing protocols. However, the rise in video conferences often results in “Zoom Fatigue” which describes the tiredness, worry, or burnout after prolonged video chats.

Learn More

Problem

The increase in the amount of eye contact people receive on Zoom calls can be stressful. Everyone is looking at each other nonstop.

Learn More

Solution

Leave 10-15 minutes between calls to take a break. Bathroom breaks do not count. Stand up and walk around.
